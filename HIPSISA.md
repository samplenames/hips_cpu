## **HIPS ISA**

**Like MIPS but hundreds of Instructions per Second!**

**A simple ISA built around the MIPS architecture with the core instructions, making it easy to understand and implement. Like MIPS, it has three instruction types: R, I, and J. The implementation is unpipelined without any forwarding, allowing for easier control logic especially for a first implementation. In the past, when I have tried to design my own CPU, I was too ambitious in what I wanted for a first time design. It caused me to never really commit to the project, but now when I break it down to simple modules, I am able to make meaningful progress everyday.**
